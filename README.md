# Anees - Therapy Connection Chatbot 🧠💬

A proof-of-concept AI chatbot built during a 2023 Hackathon. Designed as a commercial triage tool, it helps users understand their mental health needs and connects them with the right local therapists without crossing the line into providing medical advice.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Environment:** Jupyter Notebook, Python
* **Data & AI:** Custom Dataset Curation, Conversational AI

## ✨ Core Features
* **Safe Triage System:** Specifically engineered to provide condition briefs and actionable next steps without dispensing medical information.
* **Custom Conversational Model:** Trained on a proprietary dataset of 1,000+ therapist-patient conversational structures to ensure empathetic and professional routing.
* **Specialist Recommendation:** Matches users to the best local doctors and specialists based on their specific conversational inputs.

## 🧠 The Build & Challenges
**Solo Hackathon MVP:** I architected and built this entire proof-of-concept independently over a single hackathon weekend. 

**Rapid Data Engineering:** To make the AI's responses natural and effective, I spent two days collecting and curating a custom dataset of over 1,000 therapy conversations. This allowed the model to learn the *structure* of professional responses rather than just giving generic answers.

**Status:** This project serves as a foundational prototype demonstrating my ability to rapidly collect data, train a conversational model, and architect a safe, commercial health-tech solution under extreme time constraints.
